Challenge Overview
Conversational Head Generation Challenge

ViCo conversational head generation challenge is organized in conjunction with ACM Multimedia 2022. There are two tracks in our challenge:

  • Vivid Talking Head Video Generation. Given the identity and audio signals of the speaker, participants are challenged to develop an algorithm to generate a video that can match the audio signals.
  • Responsive Listening Head Video Generation. Given the identity of the listener, and a corresponding talking head video & audio, participants are challenge to develop an algorithm to generate a video that can respond to the speaker's behaviors actively in real-time.
Those generation videos are expected to be clear, lively and identity-preserved.

Individuals and teams with top submissions will present their work at the workshop.

Dataset Overview
ViCo: First Visual Conversation Dataset

All of the videos in our ViCo dataset are collected from the online video sharing platform YouTube. Rich video clips are contained in the dataset. These clips are collected from various face-to-face conversational scenarios, where bi-directional information flow is highlighted between two people. All video clips are manually checked/labeled by the production expert team.
